All About Cats 🐱

Meet the Cat

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as pets. They are known for their agility, playfulness, and independence.

Fun Facts

  • 🐾 Cats sleep for around 12–16 hours a day
  • 🐾 They can rotate their ears 180 degrees
  • 🐾 Cats use their whiskers to detect space
